My story

After discovering my love and passion for coding quite late in life, I knew I needed to change careers. I am a predominantly self-taught programmer with a background in finance and investment. I'm self-motivated, ambitious and very dependable. I am comfortable working under pressure and hitting deadlines. My main goal at this point in time is to further my knowledge and create a long term career within the tech industry.

South Africa

I was born and raised in Johannesburg, South Africa. It was there where I went to both High School and University, ultimately graduating with a finance degree from the University of the Witwatersrand. I played basketball at a very high level in university, winning national titles with the WITS team, as well as a short stint playing professionally for the Egoli Magic. Near the end of my tenure at university, I transitioned to boxing, becoming a competitive amateur and having numerous fights in both South Africa and the UK.

UK

In early 2021, in the midst of the COVID pandemic, I packed my bags and moved to London in search of opportunity and adventure. I continued my boxing career, fighting in various locations in England. I ended up entering the financial industry, working for a wealth management company. Very shorty after starting, I took an intro to python course purely because I thought it would look good on my CV. However, after one online tutorial I was hooked. At this point I realized that this is what I want to do with my life. I quit my job and began my journey of learning programming. Fast forward a few months and countless hours fully emersing myself in coding, and here we are.
